Hi, I'm Daniela! I'm 27 years old and I live in Long Beach, California. I currently am a student at Los Angeles Pacific College taking up AAS in Computer Graphic Design. I have a passion for art and design and I love translating that to digital mediums. I love to travel and explore new places, and I enjoy photography as a way to capture the beauty of the world around me.

I grew up in Mandaue City, Cebu, Philippines, and moved the United States in 2022. Cebu is know for its beautiful beaches and rich culture. Not only that, but you can also drive to the mountains in just a few hours It reminds me so much if California, where I now live. I love the diversity and vibrancy of California, and I feel at home here.
